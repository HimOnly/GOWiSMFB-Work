About Us

GOWiS Soluxions Micro-Finance Bank is a multi-purpose financial organization licensed to carry on business of providing micro-finance services such as savings, loans, domestic funds transfer, and other financial services that are needed by the economically active poor, micro, small and medium enterprises to conduct or expand their businesses as defined in the guideline for MFB in Nigeria.

The SEM Grant

Social Exchange Market ‘SEM’ is the foremost international non-governmental economic empowerment intervention towards improving the standard of living of hitherto impoverish family in all commonwealth nations, as evinced by our management dexterity, valued services, prompt response, discipline, efficient management and effective controls for accountability.

Poverty is hyper rising in most third world nations with the gap between the rich and the poor, daily widening unabated. Microfinance system in most third world nations now manages deposit rather than as channel to redistribute wealth. Funds are now practically out of reach of the poor would-be entrepreneur. Societal activities in the poor third world nation is actually further impoverishing the poor. There is no real help in-sight for the underpowered would-be entrepreneur.Social Exchange Market ‘SEM’ is mandated to bring economic succour to the poor visionary people by empowering them for low-scale people-oriented business or project for self- sustainability.

The SEM Grant (More Info)

September 1-30, 2018

Phase 1

Application

Do you have a business you run? or You want to start your business? If your answer to any of these questions is yes, then you are eligible to apply for this grant scheme. It's not a loan. It's a grant. This is a one man-one project scheme, where individuals belonging to any community in Nigeria must meet the age requirement of been at least 18 years to be qualified for this empowerment.We repeat, It is not a loan, it is a grant issued to verified members. The only payback is the profitable utilization of funds. Limited spaces are available.

October 8-12, 2018

Phase 3

Verification & Selection

This is an exercise which involves an invitation for interview where we would meet you one-on-one at our locations nearest to you for verification of your documents and business proposals. Once verification is completed and you are selected,you'll commence selection procedures to validate your membership,getting ready for disbursement.

October 1-5, 2018

Phase 2

Access & Affirmation

To gain access to this scheme, after meeting the age requirement, you must be someone who is having passion for economic revolution of a community, town or city in Nigeria.You must have a business/investment that you want to establish in a particular community, town or city and be ready to provide employment through your business opportunity. To affirm your application, you are to make a payment of #1,200 only, to our designated commercial bank account and return to this website to fill the applicant's payment form to continue your registration.

October 15, 2018

Phase 4

Disbursement

This is the stage at which all registered members only are entitled to collection of grants, between #250,000 and #10,000,000 been disbursed stage by stage based on your approval within a specified period, under close and proper monitoring, ensuring the funds available are strictly spent on the business/investment. Defaulters would be severely punished .
